## 📖 Overview

GroundSet Decision Journey is a frontend prototype created as part of the **BPOptima Forward Deployed Engineer (SLMs) Take-Home Assignment**.

The prototype reimagines how enterprise AI products can communicate complex decision-making systems through interactive storytelling instead of static marketing pages.

Inspired by Stripe's product storytelling philosophy, this experience helps visitors understand how **GroundSet** converts evidence into explainable, deterministic, and auditable decisions.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Modern enterprises store:

- 📊 Business Data (ERP)
- 👥 Customer Data (CRM)
- 💳 Financial Data (Core Banking)

But they rarely store the reasoning behind important business decisions.

GroundSet solves this by creating a **System of Record for Decisions**, ensuring every decision is transparent, explainable, and backed by a complete audit trail.
